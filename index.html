Free iOS & Android App for Vehicle Owners

Grow Your Business!

Vehicle owners can find detailers in their area and request appointments.

Branded

Upload your logo so vehicle owners see it everytime they use the app.

Fully Customizable

Customize your packages & add-ons, automate customer appointment reminders, show off your certifications & awards, capture sales tax, customer rewards and much more.

Automated Payments

Vehicle owners are charged automatically after each appointment. Charges are cleared in ~1 business day and transferred into your bank account.

Customer Notifications & Reminders

Push notifications are sent to vehicle owners when their appointment is scheduled, when their appointment is upcoming, when the detailer is on their way and other steps along the appointment process.

Detailer Notifications & Reminders

Detailers are notified as soon as new appointments are requested and reminded when an appointment is upcoming!

Scheduling

Review requested & scheduled appointments on a single calendar. Schedule with a single click! Have a scheduling conflict or bad weather? No problem, you can recommend new dates/times to the customer, they'll be notified & can reschedule seemlessly.

Customer Rewards

Kleancierge provides rewards to customers at no expense to the detailer! When customer use their rewards NO cost or price reduction is incurred by the detailer.
