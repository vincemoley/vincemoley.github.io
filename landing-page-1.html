Why you should get your vehicle detailed

Getting your vehicle detailed is like a spa day for your vehicle. Below are the top reasons to get your vehicle detailed.

Remove salt, bugs & debris that cause paint etching or worse; rust.

Remove road paint, minor paint transfer and scratches.

Remove corrosion causing brake dust and salt from wheels.

Apply a wax and/or sealant to protect the paint and create a lustrous shine.

Apply a sealant to protect the wheels and tires and make them shine like new.

Remove dust, dirt, pet hair and debris from the interior and trunk.

Treat interior stains and extract debris that cannot be removed with a vaccum.
