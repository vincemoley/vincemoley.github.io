About Kleancierge

Kleancierge is here to make your life easier and cleaner!

We do this by making it easy to schedule, receive and pay for your vehicle detailing without leaving your home or office. Simple. as. that.

This Is Our Story

As a vehicle owner, we know how tough it is to find a reputable detailer, determine the type of service(s) needed, schedule an appointment and pay. We've been through it and that's why we created Kleancierge.

We've learned how tough it is for detailers to find new customers (or be found), while providing the best service and experience possible. Being a small business owner means wearing all the hats. Now, however, Kleancierge can take care of your sales, payment processing & scheduling without any additional work on your end.
